critical_information:

architectural_decisions:

  - Three-layer architecture:
      - Ingestion
      - Normalization
      - Retrieval

  - Every provider (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) must normalize into one
    internal conversation representation.

  - Original conversations must never be overwritten.

  - Compression artifacts are stored separately from original conversations.

  - Attachments are stored in object storage while metadata remains in MongoDB.

  - Embeddings are generated asynchronously after conversation import.

  - Compression algorithms expose a common interface independent of model vendor.

  - LLM providers are abstracted behind an interface.

  - Embedding providers are abstracted independently from LLM providers.

  - Store both:
      - Raw provider export
      - Normalized conversation

  - Import parsing and validation are separate pipeline stages.

  - Parent-child message relationships support branching conversations.
